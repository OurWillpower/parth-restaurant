Our Story

The Spirit of Konkan
Lives Here

Nestled at the Titha Flyover junction, Parth RBR sits on 2 lush acres of Mango and Kaju groves — a true Konkani sanctuary. Proudly managed by women, we believe great hospitality begins at home: in the warmth of a meal cooked without shortcuts, and a room prepared with care.


Whether you're a traveler seeking a rest stop on the highway, or a local looking for authentic Konkani flavors and the finest selection of beverages, Parth RBR is your destination.
